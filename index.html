About Me

As a dedicated legal professional completing my LLB at KiiT University, I possess a fervent interest in the intricate dynamics of the legal world. My journey has been shaped by diverse, hands-on experiences—from delving into corporate law intricacies at top-tier firms to navigating the complexities of criminal litigation and supporting public law cases.

I am driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to justice. I thrive on deconstructing complex legal challenges and crafting innovative solutions. My goal is to leverage my skills in research, drafting, and advocacy to make a tangible and positive impact within the legal landscape.

My Philosophy

I believe the law is not just a set of rules, but a dynamic tool for progress and equity. My approach is founded on three pillars: meticulous preparation, creative problem-solving, and unwavering ethical integrity. I strive to understand not just the letter of the law, but its spirit and real-world impact, ensuring that my work is both legally sound and practically effective.
